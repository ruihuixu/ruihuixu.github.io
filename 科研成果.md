\title{CERTrack: Exploiting Complementary Event Representations for RGBE Tracking}

RGBE tracking leverages event cameras to enhance visual object tracking. However, most methods compress events into a single RGB-compatible representation, failing to fully exploit the abundant event information. To mitigate this limitation, we propose CERTrack, which leverages complementary event representations: high-resolution event images for spatial anchors, low-resolution voxels for temporal guides. Specifically, we introduce the Progressive Sparse Aggregator (PSA) to efficiently extract motion priors from voxels. Furthermore, we introduce the Voxel-Guided Evolver (VGE), a gated module that evolves event-image prompts layer-by-layer with voxel-derived motion priors, producing motion-aware prompts injected into the RGB backbone. Our CERTrack achieves state-of-the-art performance on VisEvent and COESOT. Ablation and exploration studies demonstrate the strategy's effectiveness and generalizability.
